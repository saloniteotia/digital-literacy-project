# BRIEF DESCRIPTION OF TASK-1

This infographic presents the key concepts of digital literacy in a simple and visually engaging manner. It explains what digital literacy means and highlights its importance in today’s technology-driven world. The content includes essential digital tools that students can use for learning and productivity, such as online collaboration and presentation platforms. It also focuses on safe internet practices, helping students understand how to protect their personal information and avoid online risks. Additionally, the infographic emphasizes the importance of maintaining a professional online presence and following proper email etiquette. Overall, it aims to create awareness and encourage responsible and effective use of digital technology among students.

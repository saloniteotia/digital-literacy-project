# SOCIAL MEDIA CHECKLIST

## Do’s (Good Practices)
* Think before you post – consider how it may affect your reputation.
* Use strong privacy settings to control who sees your content.
* Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading fake news.
* Be respectful and polite in comments and messages.
* Build a positive digital presence (professional and meaningful posts).

## Don’ts (Things to Avoid)
* Don’t share personal information (address, phone number, passwords).
* Don’t engage in cyberbullying or trolling.
* Don’t accept friend requests from unknown people.
* Don’t overshare location or live updates publicly.
* Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content.

# TASK-5

## PART-B : PREVENTION CHECKLIST

STAY SAFE ONLINE

1) Enable 2-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps.

2) Use strong, unique passwords for each account (use a mix of letters, numbers, symbols).

3) Ensure you log out from your accounts on shared computers (college labs, cyber cafés).

4) Be cautious while sharing your personal info (address, phone, college ID) on social media.

5) Never share your OTP, PIN, or UPI collect approval with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank. Remember, scanning a QR code sends money, it does NOT receive money.

6) Ensure the receiver's name is correct before confirming the transaction.

7) If Something Feels Wrong: Immediately block your bank account/UPI app if you suspect fraud. Save screenshots and evidence of suspicious messages.

8) Report Cybercrime Immediately:

   National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in

   National Cyber Crime  Helpline: 1930

# TASK-5

## PART-B : PREVENTION CHECKLIST

### STAY SAFE ONLINE

* Enable 2-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps.

*  Use strong, unique passwords for each account (use a mix of letters, numbers, symbols).

* Ensure you log out from your accounts on shared computers (college labs, cyber cafés).

* Be cautious while sharing your personal info (address, phone, college ID) on social media.

* Never share your OTP, PIN, or UPI collect approval with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank. Remember, scanning a QR code sends money, it does NOT receive money.

* Ensure the receiver's name is correct before confirming the transaction.

* If Something Feels Wrong immediately block your bank account/UPI app if you suspect fraud and save screenshots and evidence of suspicious messages.

* Download apps only from official stores (Play Store/App Store).

#### Report Cybercrime Immediately:

   National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
